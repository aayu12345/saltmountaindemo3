About Salt Mountain Properties

Your Gateway to the Backwaters

Welcome to Salt Mountain Properties! Founded by Mike, Tim, and Mitch, we are a Veteran-Owned company dedicated to providing exceptional experiences and value for both our guests and property owners. With Mike’s expertise as a North Carolina Broker in Charge, we bring a wealth of knowledge and professionalism to the hospitality industry.

Our name, Salt Mountain Properties, reflects the stunning properties we manage in the breathtaking North Carolina Mountains and along the beautiful beaches of North and South Carolina. We are passionate about creating memorable stays in these picturesque locations.

To ensure a quality experience, we’ve established a strong infrastructure supported by dedicated employees at each of our locations. Our team is committed to delivering outstanding service and maintaining the highest standards.

In addition to catering to our guests, we also focus on the needs of property owners. We offer comprehensive short-term property management and cleaning services designed to maximize the potential of your investment.

Thank you for choosing Salt Mountain Properties. We look forward to serving you and making your experience unforgettable!
